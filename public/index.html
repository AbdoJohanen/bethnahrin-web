About Us

Kooperativet Beth-Nahrin develops digital solutions that strengthen culture, education, and community engagement. Our current projects focus on language accessibility, but our mission extends to creating modern tools that support learning, connection, and cultural continuity in the digital era.

Our Mission

To develop meaningful digital products that preserve knowledge, support education, and empower communities. We build tools that make learning accessible, starting with language, but never limited to it.

Our Values

We value innovation, accessibility, and cultural respect. Our work combines modern technology with deep care for the traditions, identities, and communities we aim to support.

Our Vision

To create a digital ecosystem where endangered knowledge, languages, and cultural expressions can thrive, not only preserved, but revitalized for future generations through engaging, user-friendly technologies.
