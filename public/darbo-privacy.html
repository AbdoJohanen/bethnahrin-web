Darbo Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 17, 2025

Darbo, developed by Kooperativet Beth-Nahrin, is a free educational and entertainment app designed for all ages.

Information Collection and Use

Darbo does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. The app functions entirely on your device and does not transmit data to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Darbo is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children.

Permissions

Darbo does not request any special device permissions beyond those required for basic app functionality (e.g., playing sounds, displaying images).

Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties, nor does the app contain third-party advertising.

Security

Since Darbo does not collect or transmit any user data, there are no data security concerns related to information storage or transmission.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@bethnahrin.se.
